Just some tips to get you started!

  • A shortcut: for Windows, press Win + space to quickly get to the Chinese keyboard.
  • Another shortcut: to get Chinese punctuation: press Ctrl + Alt + ,
  • The best way to improve is to sound out the words. For example, practice saying a ao e and so forth.
  • Make sure the word is correct before you press enter.
  • If it is a common phrase, it may be easier to find the words after you type them all.
  • If you don't know how to read the word, ask for help first. Then you will be able to type.
  • Remember, practice makes perfect! So practice, practice, practice!

Keep calm and type on ;)
