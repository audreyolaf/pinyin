Instructions Tips

To set up your Chinese hanyu pinyin on your computer, follow these steps.

  1. On windows, open settings.
  2. Then find 'Time & language'.
  3. Select 'language'.
  4. Then click 'Add a language'.
  5. Choose 中文(台灣).
  6. Once it is installed, click on it and click 'Options'.
  7. Click 'Add a keyboard'.
  8. Add Microsoft Bopomofo.
  9. Once it is installed, click into 'Options'.
  10. Scroll until you see 'Keyboard layouts', and change it to 'Hanyu Pinyin'.
